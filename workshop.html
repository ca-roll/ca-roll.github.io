CAROLL Workshop

The 1st CAROLL Workshop will take place at the University of Passau on 31.03.2023, co-located with the 5th Passau Data Science Summit (PasDaS).

A great line-up of International speakers will present their current work on hot NLP topics, relaxed discussions are planned to be had in our poster session, and a panel discussion about all things NLP will spice up the entire event.

Invited Speakers:

  • Prof. Stavros Assimakopoulos (University of Malta)
  • Prof. Valerio Basile (University of Turin)
  • Prof. Tommaso Caselli (University of Groningen)
  • Prof. Randy Allen Harris (University of Waterloo)
  • Dr. David Herel (Czech Institute of Informatics)
  • Dr. Yetian Wang (University of Waterloo)
